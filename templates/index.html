What is Procxam?

Procxam is a face-recognition-based website for managing and tracking attendance, preventing the proxy in online tests and meetings. It helps teachers to conduct online tests and meetings more efficiently and securely. It uses face recognition technology to verify the student's authenticity by capturing real-time images and checking the record.

Get Started

HOST A TEST

This feature gives users access to host tests on the Procxam platform, which helps maintain the test integrity and make it more secure through our face recognition technology while assuring that the students experience is exceptional at each step.

HOST A MEETING

This feature gives users access to conduct online meetings securely. It maintains the decorum and integrity of the meeting by creating an environment where communication is accessible, and all invitees feel included and safe to speak.
